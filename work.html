Michigan Institute for Data Science (MIDAS)
Ann Arbor, MI

2020

Web Developer  While working at MIDAS, I was responsible for redesigning many pages to bring a more consistent, modern look and feel to their whole website. From the Homepage to the Mood of America page, I was dedicated to creating dynamic, functional, and beautiful webpages. I continued to build dynamic solutions for MIDAS by transforming the static Reproducibility Resources and Research Datasets pages into robust, easily sustainable pages.
